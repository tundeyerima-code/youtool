Our Mission

In an era of information overload, video content is a goldmine that is often locked behind hours of playback. TubeLogic AI was built to be your digital librarian.

By leveraging the Google Gemini 1.5 Flash model, we provide instantaneous transcription and synthesis, allowing researchers, students, and content marketers to transform video data into actionable text assets.

Why it matters?

  • Saves 80% of manual note-taking time.
  • Generates SEO-ready blog posts from video transcripts.
  • Provides accessibility for the hearing impaired.
